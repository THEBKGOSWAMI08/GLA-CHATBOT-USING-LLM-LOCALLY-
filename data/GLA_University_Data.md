# GLA University, Mathura - Comprehensive Information

## Overview
**GLA University** is a private university located in Mathura, Uttar Pradesh, India. It was established in 2010 through the U.P. State Legislative Act of 2009. The university is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and accredited with an **'A+' grade by NAAC**.

*   **Motto:** "Education is the manifestation of perfection already existing in man" (Swami Vivekananda)
*   **Location:** 17km Stone, NH-2, Mathura-Delhi Road, P.O. Chaumuhan, Mathura-281 406 (U.P.), INDIA
*   **Chancellor:** Mr. Narayan Das Agrawal
*   **Vice-Chancellor:** Prof. Phalguni Gupta

## Key Highlights
*   **Accreditation:** NAAC A+ Grade (3.46/4)
*   **NIRF Ranking:** Consistently ranked among top universities in India (Pharmacy: 50-75 band, Engineering: 100-150 band often).
*   **Placement:** Excellent placement record with highest packages reaching above ₹50 LPA.
*   **Campus:** Sprawling 110+ acre green campus.

## Institutes and Departments
GLA University comprises several institutes offering a wide range of programs:
1.  **Institute of Engineering and Technology (IET):** CSE, ECE, ME, CE, EE, etc.
2.  **Institute of Business Management (IBM):** MBA, BBA, B.Com (Hons).
3.  **Institute of Pharmaceutical Research (IPR):** B.Pharm, M.Pharm.
4.  **Institute of Applied Sciences & Humanities:** BSc, MSc (Physics, Chemistry, Maths, Biotech).
5.  **Institute of Legal Studies & Research:** BA-LLB, BBA-LLB.
6.  **University Polytechnic:** Diploma in Engineering.
7.  **Department of Agriculture:** BSc (Hons) Agriculture.

## Placements
GLA University is renowned for its placements, especially in the Engineering and Management sectors.
*   **Top Recruiters:** Microsoft, Amazon, Capgemini, Accenture, TCS, Wipro, Infosys, HDFC Bank, etc.
*   **Highest Package:** Has historically crossed **₹55 LPA**.
*   **Average Package:** Around **₹5 - 6.5 LPA** for engineering.
*   **Placement Cell:** The Training & Placement (T&P) cell is very active and provides soft skills training, mock interviews, and industry connect programs.

## Campus Facilities
*   **Hostels:** Separate AC and Non-AC hostels for boys and girls with mess facilities, gym, and common rooms.
*   **Library:** Central library with thousands of books, journals, and e-recourses.
*   **Laboratories:** State-of-the-art labs for all departments.
*   **Sports:** Facilities for Cricket, Football, Basketball, Volleyball, and indoor games.
*   **Medical:** On-campus dispensary and ambulance service.
*   **Transport:** Bus facility covering Mathura, Vrindavan, Agra, and nearby areas.

## Admission Process
1.  **Online Application:** Fill the form on the official website (gla.ac.in).
2.  **Entrance Test:** Appear for **GLAET** (GLA Entrance Test), an online MCQ-based exam.
3.  **Result & Counseling:** Based on GLAET score (or JEE Mains/CUET/MAT depending on course), candidates are called for counseling.
4.  **Document Verification & Fee Submission:** Confirm seat by paying fees.
